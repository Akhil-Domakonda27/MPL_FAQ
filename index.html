FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the team size? What if we don't have enough team members?
The team size is 3. If you don’t have enough team members we will make groups for you.
When will we get certificates?
The certificates will be provided by the graVITas committee, it will be provided within few days of culmination of the fest.
On which platform will we conduct the event?
Due to the pandemic the event will be conducted online on Microsoft teams.
How will we communicate with team members?
You can communicate through Microsoft teams or can even exchange WhatsApp number depending upon your comfortability.
What level of difficulty will the questions be considering the fact that school students will be competing against college students?
The questions will be based majorly on high school mathematics, aptitude and logical reasoning.
I have registered and paid for the event, but I haven't received any confirmation. Whom should I contact?
You can contact the event's coordinator. The details have been mentioned on the event’s website as well as on the graVITas website.
How can the school students register for the event?
The external participants can register as non-vitians in the registration portal of gravitas website.
